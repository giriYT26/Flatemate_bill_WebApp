Welcome to Flatmate Bill Splitter

About This Page

This web application helps flatmates easily calculate and split their household bills based on the number of days each person has stayed in the house.
Simply fill in the bill amount, the billing period, and the number of days each flatmate stayed. The app will calculate how much each person needs to contribute.

In Short

In this webapp we can split the bill between two room mates
